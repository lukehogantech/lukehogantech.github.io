About Me

Cybersecurity, cloud and AI enablement consultant with 4 years' experience delivering Microsoft funded security and infrastructure assessments across enterprise, government, corporate and education sectors. I specialise in helping organisations strengthen their security posture, reduce risk, and unlock the full value of Microsoft 365, Azure, and emerging AI technologies.

As a consultant on Microsoft's ANZ Solution Assessment desk, I lead customers through high-impact assessments across Threat Protection, Data Security, Azure Benchmark TCO, and Security Business Value Assessments (BVA). I'm the primary consultant driving security-focused engagements and serve as a subject matter expert for key tools and assessment offerings. I blend deep technical expertise with strategic consulting to drive measurable business outcomes for Microsoft's customers and partners, including modernisation, cost optimisation, compliance uplift and AI-driven productivity gains.

I thrive in roles that blend hands-on technical work with strategic thinking. My strengths lie in cloud architecture, security frameworks (Essential Eight, CIS v8), and translating complex findings into clear, actionable insights for stakeholders.

In my current role, I provide technical leadership and strategic input across complex solution assessments. I operate with a high degree of autonomy and ownership and serve as a trusted consultant for high-impact client engagements. I’m focused on continuing to grow in cybersecurity architecture, AI governance and adoption, and cloud solution design, driving innovation while staying hands-on with the technology. I’m also open to leveraging my executive leadership experience on projects or mentoring teams where the opportunity aligns with a collaborative culture and technical depth.
